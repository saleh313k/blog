Who am I?

My name is Saleh Khademi and I was born in Shiraz,Iran, 5th. Novembre 1991. When I developed a web site for the first time, I was at primary school. It was a static page for my school and I used pure HTML
My major in highschool was computer and I was interested in Robotics in that time, so I joined to robotic team in my highschool. I passed about two years in software engineering in Bandar-Abbas university and after that I continued my education in Shiraz university.
I started my proffessional activities from Soshiant company in 2008 when I was working as a industrial programmer. We developed a firmware for CNC machines using C language, after that I worked for an online robotic shop as a manager, finally I changed my line of work from robotics and smart machines to software development with alot of experience in 3D printing, robotics and smart systems(IoT). In the last five years, I have worked for some small and large businesses as developer, designer and team & project manager. Honestly, nowadays I enjoy freelancing and consulting for businesses, companies and industries
My hobby is programming, specially web and mobile apps, but I like to taste another amusements. I really like Tennis and play whenever I have pleasure time, becuase this is my favorite sport. In Addition I do Yoga two times a week and also usually use my bike to do my daily activities.
